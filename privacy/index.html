Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 May 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 May 2026

ScoreAU is built with privacy in mind. We collect only what's necessary to provide live sports scores, personalised alerts, and cross-device sync. We don't sell your data.

Who we are

ScoreAU is operated by Adrian McGee ("we", "us", "our"), based in Australia. You can reach us at scoreappau@gmail.com.

This policy applies to the ScoreAU iOS application and the backend services that power it. It is governed by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles.

What we collect

Account information

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

  • Email address (if provided via email registration or Google Sign-In)
  • Display name (optional, if provided)
  • A unique identifier from Apple or Google if you use Sign in with Apple / Google

An account is entirely optional. You can use ScoreAU anonymously — no sign-in is required to view scores.

App preferences and settings

  • Teams you follow
  • Notification preferences (pre-game lead time, alert types)
  • Display preferences (hide scores, selected sport filter)

These are stored on your device and, if you are signed in, synced to our server so they persist across reinstalls and devices.

Device tokens

To deliver push notifications and Live Activity updates, we store your device's APNs (Apple Push Notification service) token on our server. This token is used only to send you notifications and is not shared with third parties for any other purpose.

Usage analytics

We use TelemetryDeck for privacy-first analytics. TelemetryDeck does not collect personally identifiable information and processes events anonymously on their servers. Data collected includes: app launches, feature usage, tab selections, and crash-related signals. No advertising identifiers are used.

Crash reports

We use Sentry to receive automatic crash reports. Crash reports may contain device information (OS version, device model) and a stack trace of the error. They do not contain your account details or the content of your notifications.

How we use your information

  • To deliver personalised live score alerts and Live Activities for teams you follow
  • To sync your followed teams and preferences across your devices
  • To process and manage your Pro subscription via Apple's App Store
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability
  • To understand which features are used so we can prioritise improvements

We do not use your data for targeted advertising and we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Third-party services

ScoreAU fetches sports data from the following public APIs. Your personal information is not sent to these services:

  • ESPN (sports scores and statistics)
  • football-data.org (EPL scores)
  • Squiggle (AFL data)
  • API-Football / The Odds API (A-League, supplementary data)
  • CricketData.org (BBL data)

Sign-in is processed by Apple (Sign in with Apple) and Google (Google Sign-In). Please refer to their respective privacy policies for how they handle authentication.

Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Data retention

  • Account data — retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Device tokens — retained while your device is registered. Expired tokens are removed automatically.
  • Analytics — TelemetryDeck retains anonymised signals for up to 90 days.
  • Crash reports — Sentry retains reports for 90 days by default.

Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of analytics at any time by adjusting your device's privacy settings

To delete your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account in the app, or email us at scoreappau@gmail.com.

Children's privacy

ScoreAU is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including TLS encryption in transit, JWT-based authentication with short-lived access tokens, and hashed refresh tokens stored in our database. No method of transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of ScoreAU after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes, we'll notify you via the app.
