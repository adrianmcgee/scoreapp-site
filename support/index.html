Frequently Asked Questions

How do I follow my favourite teams?

Open the app and tap the star icon next to any team to add them to your favourites. Their matches will appear at the top of your feed.

Why aren't live scores updating?

Make sure you have a stable internet connection. Pull down on the match list to refresh. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

Can I change my notification settings?

Yes, go to Settings > Notifications within the app to customise which alerts you receive for your favourite teams.

Which sports are supported?

ScoreAU currently covers AFL, NRL, A-League, Super Rugby, Cricket, and more Australian sports with live scores and fixtures.
